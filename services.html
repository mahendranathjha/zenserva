Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about our services and engagement models.

What industries do you serve?

We work with manufacturing, logistics, retail, and education across India, delivering scalable tech solutions tailored to each sector.

Do you serve clients outside Noida?

Yes. We support clients nationwide and offer remote consulting, with on-site teams where required.

What is the typical ERP implementation timeline?

Timelines vary by scope, but most mid-size ERP projects take 8-16 weeks from discovery to go-live with a structured rollout.

Do you provide ongoing support after deployment?

Yes. We offer monthly retainers, SLAs, and 24/7 support for high-priority systems.

How do we start a project with Zenserva?

Contact us to schedule a discovery call, share goals, and we will draft a tailored proposal and implementation plan.
